About Saroja Hospital, Wari – Prayagraj

Welcome to Saroja Hospital — a trusted healthcare center in Wari, Prayagraj dedicated to advanced medical care, expert treatment, and compassionate patient service.

Providing Quality & Affordable Healthcare in Prayagraj

Saroja Hospital is committed to delivering comprehensive healthcare services with excellence and integrity. Located in Wari, Prayagraj, we specialize in Endoscopy, Colonoscopy, Laparoscopic Surgery, and Chest & Respiratory Care using the latest medical technology.

Our mission is to make quality medical treatment accessible to every individual. From routine check-ups to advanced surgeries, our experienced team ensures personalized care in a safe and supportive environment.
