Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Saroja Hospital collects, uses, stores and protects your personal and medical information in accordance with applicable healthcare and data protection laws.

Effective Date: 27 February 2025

Privacy Policy of Saroja Hospital

At Saroja Hospital, we are committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of our patients, visitors and website users. This policy outlines how we handle personal, medical and contact information collected through our services and website.

1. Introduction

Saroja Hospital respects your privacy and is committed to safeguarding your personal and medical information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose and protect information when you visit our website, book appointments or receive medical services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal & Medical Information

  • Name, age, gender and contact details
  • Medical history and treatment-related information
  • Appointment and consultation details
  • Payment or billing information, where applicable

2.2 Website Usage Information

  • IP address and browser type
  • Pages visited and interaction data
  • Device and technical information

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide medical consultation and treatment services
  • To manage appointments and patient communication
  • To improve healthcare services and patient experience
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • To ensure safety, security and fraud prevention

4. Information Sharing & Disclosure

Saroja Hospital does not sell or rent your personal or medical information. Information may be shared only with:

  • Authorized medical professionals involved in your care
  • Diagnostic labs or service providers as required for treatment
  • Government or legal authorities when required by law

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and administrative security measures to protect personal and medical information against unauthorized access, loss or misuse.
